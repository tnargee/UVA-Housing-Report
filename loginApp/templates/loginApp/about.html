Housing Reporting

This site is a University-centered Housing Reporting app.

We created this site during the Spring of 2024 in CS 3240 at the University of Virginia.

Why a Housing Reporting App?

A University of Virginia (UVA) housing reporting app is a crucial tool needed to enhance the safety and well-being of students residing on campus. Currently, there is no streamlined method for students to quickly and effectively report housing issues, whether they be maintenance problems, safety concerns, or violations of housing policies. By implementing a dedicated app, UVA can ensure that students have a direct and efficient way to communicate their housing-related incidents. This not only expedites the resolution process but also promotes a proactive approach to maintaining the quality and security of student accommodations. Such a platform would centralize reports, track response times, and ultimately foster a safer living environment for all students.
